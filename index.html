About Us

The Knowledge Computing Group was established in 2020 at the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. We focus on cutting-edge research in knowledge computing, data science, machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision. Our mission is to advance the field of artificial intelligence and cultivate outstanding research talent.

Our group consists of 2 professors, 3 associate professors, 2 lecturers, 15 PhD students, and 30 master's students. We have established strong collaborative relationships with renowned universities and enterprises both domestically and internationally, and have undertaken numerous national and provincial-level research projects.
