Research Areas

Exploring cutting-edge technologies in knowledge computing and artificial intelligence

Knowledge Graphs

We focus on knowledge graph construction, reasoning, and applications across various domains. Our research includes:

  • Knowledge graph construction and completion
  • Graph neural networks and reasoning
  • Knowledge graph embedding and representation learning
  • Multi-modal knowledge graphs

Natural Language Processing

We develop advanced NLP technologies that enable computers to understand and generate human language. Our research includes:

  • Large language models and pre-training
  • Machine translation and multilingual processing
  • Text mining and sentiment analysis
  • Dialogue systems and question answering

Big Data Knowledge Engineering

In the era of big data, we research how to extract valuable knowledge from massive datasets. Our research includes:

  • Large-scale knowledge extraction and processing
  • Knowledge discovery from big data
  • Distributed knowledge management systems
  • Real-time knowledge processing and analytics

Knowledge Computing

We develop knowledge-based intelligent systems and reasoning mechanisms. Our research includes:

  • Knowledge representation and reasoning
  • Knowledge-based question answering
  • Automated knowledge acquisition
  • Knowledge-enhanced machine learning
